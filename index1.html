Advancing Equity in Maryland's Cannabis Industry

Fast Facts

  • 174 social equity cannabis licenses awarded in 2024 out of 1,515 applicants.
  • 175,000 cannabis convictions pardoned in June 2024, including for paraphernalia possession.
  • Disparities in cannabis business ownership still persist across Maryland.

Call to Action

  • Push for tax revenue reinvestment in communities harmed by cannabis criminalization.
  • Advocate for accessible licensing and startup funding for minority entrepreneurs.
  • Support organizations working on expungement and reentry services.
